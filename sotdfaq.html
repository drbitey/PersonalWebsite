#1: The SOTD hasn't been updated today. How often are updates?

I aim to update the song of the day each day but sometimes I forget or am otherwise unable to update. Generally the maximum delay on updates is a day, but sometimes it can be as much as 4-5 days. All days will eventually get uploaded.

#2: Can an artist/song be repeated?

The only rule I keep for the song of the day is that a song cannot be used for more than one unique day. This is to encourage diversity of the music. However, I do not have a policy against repeating artists. As of the writing of this page, Sufjan Stevens has the most entries, at 6.

#3: Can I suggest a song?

Sure! I'm always open to listening to new music. I prefer not to give out my contact information, but most people who view this site are real-life friends of mine. If you have contact with me, feel free to shoot me a song anytime.

#4: What is the objective of this site?

I started it for fun in February 2022 just to document what I was listening to. After I had a few entries, I realized I could use it to create some cool maps and diversify my music taste at the same time. See the charts link on the main page for some info on that!
